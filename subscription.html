Cancellation and ownership

What happens if you cancel?

If you cancel after your 12-month minimum term, the website will be taken offline and we'll stop hosting it. Because the subscription covers the build cost spread over time, you don't own the code unless you've exercised the buyout option.

We think that's a fair arrangement. You're paying significantly less upfront in exchange for a longer commitment. But we want to be straight with you about how it works before you sign up.

The buyout option

After your 12-month minimum term, you can buy full ownership of your site's code for a single payment equal to 3 months of your plan fee.

  • Starter £177
  • Business £267
  • Professional £417

After that, the site is yours outright. No more monthly payments, no more obligations.

FAQs

Questions about subscriptions

Is there a setup fee?

No. The monthly fee covers everything: the build, the launch, and ongoing hosting and maintenance. There's nothing extra to pay to get started.

What's included in "minor content edits"?

Text changes, swapping a photo, updating your contact details, adding a new service to your list. Anything that takes us under 30 minutes. Larger changes like adding a new page (£199) or a booking form (£149) are quoted separately.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes. You can move from Starter to Business or Business to Professional at any time. Your new monthly rate applies from the next billing date and we'll build the additional pages within 14 days.

What if I want more than 8 pages?

Additional pages can be added to any plan for £199 each as a one-off. Alternatively, consider our one-off Professional package if you need a larger site from the start.

Do you handle domain registration?

We connect and manage your existing domain as part of every plan. If you need a new domain registered, we'll sort that for you. Domain renewal costs are passed on at cost with no markup.

What happens after the 12-month minimum term?

Your subscription continues on a rolling monthly basis. You can cancel with 30 days notice at any point after month 12. If you cancel, the site goes offline. If you want to keep the site and own it outright, you can exercise the buyout option from month 12 onwards for a one-off payment equal to 3 months of your plan fee.
