FAQs

Common Questions

How long does a website take to build?

Typically 1–3 weeks depending on the package and how quickly you can supply content. We'll agree a timeline before we start.

Do I need to provide content and photos?

Yes — you know your business better than we do. We'll tell you exactly what we need. If you need help with copy, we offer a copywriting add-on.

Will my site work on mobile?

Every site we build is mobile-first. It will look and work perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Can you update my existing website?

If it's a site we built, yes — that's what our monthly retainer covers. For sites built elsewhere, get in touch and we'll assess it.

Do I own the website?

Completely. The code is yours. The domain is yours. You're not locked into any platform or paying us to keep the lights on unless you choose our retainer.

What do I need to get started?

Just get in touch. We'll have a quick call, understand what you need, and send you a written quote within 24 hours.

Not Sure Which Package?

Get in touch and we'll recommend the right fit for your business — no pressure, no jargon.
