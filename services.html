FAQs

Common Questions

Do I own the website when it's finished?

Yes, completely. The code is yours. The domain is yours. No platform lock-in, no monthly fees to keep it live — unless you choose our care retainer.

What do I need to supply?

Your logo, written content for each page, and any photos you want to use. We provide a content checklist when you confirm. The 14-day clock starts when we receive everything.

What if I don't have a logo or copy?

We offer wordmark logo design for £199 and copywriting for £125 per page. We can also use a text-based placeholder while we build and swap the logo in at no extra charge.

What does 'hand-coded' actually mean?

Every line of HTML and CSS is written by hand from a blank file. No WordPress, no Wix, no template underneath. The result is faster, cleaner, and easier for Google to read.

What happens after the site goes live?

You receive your files and the site is live on your domain. If you want ongoing support, our monthly care plans cover that. If not, you're free to manage independently.
