Questions about your builder website

Do you understand what builders need from a website?

I work exclusively with trades and construction businesses. For builders, I know the website needs to do two things above everything else: showcase your portfolio convincingly, and make it easy for customers to get in touch. Every builder site I build is structured around those two goals.

Will my builder website show up on Google?

Every site I build includes on-page SEO as standard, including correct title tags, meta descriptions, schema markup, and a site structure Google can crawl properly. I also set up Google Analytics on Business and Professional packages so you can track your rankings. Appearing on page one takes time and depends on competition in your area, but the technical foundations are right from day one.

Can you build a project portfolio section?

Yes, and for builders it's the most important part of the site. A gallery or case study section is included as an additional page on Starter and above. On Business and Professional packages, I can build individual case study pages for your biggest projects, with before-and-after photos and project descriptions.

Can you target specific project types like extensions or loft conversions?

Yes. On Business and Professional packages, high-value project types can have their own dedicated pages such as "loft conversion [town]" and "kitchen extension [town]", targeting those specific searches directly.

How quickly can you build my website?

Every package includes a 14-day delivery guarantee. The clock starts the day I receive all your content (logo, copy, and any photos). I'll send you a clear checklist of everything I need before I begin.

Do I own the website when it's finished?

Yes, completely. The code is yours. The domain is yours. You're not locked into any platform or paying a monthly fee just to keep the lights on. If you ever want to move or make changes yourself, you can. No strings attached.
